## Meeting Notes

### Opening Remarks & General Updates
- Adam began the meeting with informal conversation, welcoming everyone and confirming attendance.  
- Weather-related small talk broke the ice before transitioning into community updates.  
- Adam officially opened the November 2025 SIG Community Monthly Sync and confirmed it was the second-to-last call for the year.  

### Community Budget & Program Restructuring
- Due to budget reductions, no Q4 community governance proposal was submitted.  
- Current programs (Vanguards, Ring Masters, Navigators) remain active but operate at a smaller scale.  
- Overclock Labs now covers community expenses directly, prompting closer review and stricter allocation of funds.  
- Adam emphasized that reduced funding presents an opportunity to refine initiatives, focus on quality, and maintain manageable workloads.  

### Insiders Program (Ambassadors)
- The Insiders Program serves as Akash‚Äôs entry-level ambassador initiative.  
- Membership decreased from ~60 to 44 active insiders due to low engagement.  
- Adam now requires participants to submit a quarterly feedback form.  
- Members who failed to respond or contribute were removed, opening space for new applicants (averaging 1‚Äì2 applications per day).  
- The result has been a noticeable increase in engagement and activity among remaining members.  

### Vanguards Program
- The Vanguards, responsible for technical support on Discord, were reduced from 10 to 3 paid members.  
- Despite the reduction, most continue contributing voluntarily, demonstrating strong community commitment.  
- Adam hopes to restore funding and onboard more paid Vanguards in 2026.  

### Ring Masters Program
- Ring Masters organize local events, meetups, and hackathons.  
- Funding requests now require detailed proposals with clear goals and expected outcomes.  
- Only insiders can apply for event sponsorships.  
- Adam is currently reviewing 5‚Äì6 pending applications and asked members to follow up via Discord for updates.  

### Navigators Program
- Navigators function as business development and integrations leads.  
- With the recent hiring of a Head of Sales at Overclock Labs, the Navigators program is temporarily reduced.  
- The goal is to expand once inbound requests for GPU access and integrations increase.  

### Alchemists Program
- The Alchemists are community content creators contributing to Akash EDU.  
- Seven community-created courses are already live, with excellent feedback.  
- There remains a small budget for additional curriculum development.  
- Interested contributors must be part of the Insiders Program to qualify for funding or collaboration.  

### New Initiatives
- **Regional Leads Program:** Initially designed with its own budget, now transitioned into a volunteer-driven initiative.  
  - Regional Leads will represent Akash locally, support community onboarding, and coordinate activities.  
  - Recruitment has begun, with 1‚Äì2 volunteers expected to onboard in the coming weeks.  
- **Red Team Program:** A newly launched social media engagement initiative aimed at expanding Akash‚Äôs presence across Twitter and other platforms.  
  - Members act as ‚Äúdigital advocates,‚Äù engaging influencers and discussions to highlight Akash.  
  - Currently has 9 members, open to both insiders and general community participants.  
  - Adam compared its spirit to the LINK Marines and XRP Army, focused on organic advocacy and brand amplification.  

### Governance and Proposals Discussion
- Germany Beal raised concerns about how Overclock‚Äôs internal budget cuts might affect governance proposals.  
- Adam clarified that Overclock‚Äôs reductions apply only to its internal programs.  
- Community governance proposals remain independent and can still be proposed and voted on-chain.  
- Future proposals will face closer scrutiny to ensure alignment with Overclock‚Äôs direction and Akash‚Äôs roadmap.  
- Germany suggested improving alignment and communication about Overclock‚Äôs high-level priorities to guide contributors.  
- Adam recommended reviewing the Akash product roadmap on the official site to understand Overclock‚Äôs development priorities and open collaboration opportunities.  

### Community Engagement & Future Direction
- Adam encouraged members to join the Insiders Program to gain structured involvement and direct communication.  
- Emphasis on creating programs that are mutually beneficial‚Äîhelping participants grow personally and professionally while advancing Akash.  
- The goal is to maintain an active, skilled core of contributors rather than broad but shallow participation.  

### Events and Representation

- The final event of the year will be NeurIPS (Machine Learning Conference), featuring:  
  - A dedicated Akash workspace  
  - Coffee trucks for networking  
  - Developer engagement sessions  
- More updates will follow after NeurIPS concludes in early December.  

### Community Project Update ‚Äî Germany Beal

  - Germany welcomed community members to reach out on Discord for collaboration or testing access.  

### Closing Remarks
