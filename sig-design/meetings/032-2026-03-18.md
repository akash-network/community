### Opening Remarks & SIG Design Overview

### Website Design Updates & UX Improvements

* Denis Lelic presented recent updates to the Akash website
* Homepage updated with new hero section tiles guiding users to key actions
* Tiles direct users toward deploying on Akash ML, browsing templates, and becoming a provider
* Improvement designed to create clearer user journeys and flexibility in navigation
* Pricing page enhanced with interactive elements allowing users to adjust GPU models and usage inputs
* Pricing dynamically updates based on user selections, improving usability and clarity
* Website content reduced and restructured to lower cognitive load for users
* Information now distributed more effectively instead of presenting dense content blocks
* Community page updated to include Akash Club and allow users to navigate between interest groups
* Improvement enhances discoverability of community initiatives and participation opportunities

### Akash Home Node & Product Pages

* Soft launch of Akash Home Node landing page introduced
* Page designed as a one-pager to educate users on the product and capture early adopters
* Includes explanation of product functionality, target users, and FAQs
* Features interactive earning estimation based on consumer hardware
* Includes form for early access registration to capture interested users
* Strategy focused on validating demand and onboarding early users ahead of full launch

### Website Pipeline & Content Strategy

* Multiple new pages in development including comparison pages with competing platforms
* Dedicated pages planned for different tools within the Akash ecosystem
* Expansion of use case pages targeting specific audiences such as AI builders, developers, and startups
* Exploration of referral program pages to support growth initiatives
* Focus currently on refining copy before moving into full development
* Emphasis on aligning messaging with target audiences and use cases

### Event Branding & Design Initiatives

* Upcoming work includes design and branding for Akash Accelerate
* Full event branding strategy to be developed after internal alignment
* Plans to share designs with the community once finalized
* Recognition that event branding plays a key role in ecosystem visibility and engagement

### Product Design & Console Strategy

* Joshua Meyer shared updates on console and product design direction
* Shift toward a data-driven design approach across products
* Ongoing audit to identify gaps in tracking and analytics
* Goal to use data insights to inform feature decisions and experiments
* Development of a more structured and formalized user research process
* Plan to leverage community participation in testing and feedback

### Onboarding & User Experience Improvements

* Early-stage redesign of onboarding experience for new users
* Identification of friction points through data analysis
* Internal discussions ongoing with plans to present designs in future meetings
* Focus on improving initial user experience to increase activation and retention

### Deployment Management & Feature Improvements

* Deployment management identified as a key area of improvement
* Exploration of new features to enhance usability and control
* Goal to simplify workflows and improve overall user experience

### Model Deprecation & UX Considerations

* Addressing challenges around model deprecation in Akash ML
* Need for a smooth transition experience when models are updated or removed
* Focus on minimizing disruption and guiding users to alternative models
* Emphasis on clarity and ease of migration for users

### Cross-Product Integration

* Strategic focus on bridging Akash ML and console
* Goal to improve cross-product navigation and usage
* Aim to keep users within the ecosystem through seamless integration
* Focus on simplifying workflows across products

### Home Node Experience & Conversion Optimization

* Iteration ongoing on installation and setup experience for Home Node
* Goal to achieve near one-click setup for users
* Focus on maximizing conversion during beta and full launch
* Plan to collect and utilize feedback from early adopters
* Emphasis on refining experience before broader release
