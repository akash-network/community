### Community Feedback and Design Discussions

#### Console User Experience Feedback

* Sandeep raised concerns about usability issues in the Console interface.
* Difficulty locating deployments when many deployments exist
* Excessive space usage for individual deployment entries
* Missing deployment name visibility in some scenarios
* Certificate storage limited to the browser environment

£### Certificate Management Concerns

* Certificates stored in the browser prevent users from accessing deployments across different browsers or incognito sessions.
* Users must recreate certificates to regain access.
* Store certificates or related authentication data in backend systems for managed wallets.
