* Tyler Wright acknowledged that Burn Mint Equilibrium had been a major discussion topic across previous SIG Economics meetings and broader community governance discussions

### Blockworks Research Dashboard Update

* Tyler Wright provided a follow-up update regarding the Blockworks proposal that passed on-chain during summer 2025
* Explained that Blockworks is a research organization known for investor-focused analytics, dashboards, and industry research
* Blockworks originally proposed creating analytics and storytelling dashboards for the Akash ecosystem
* Tyler Wright shared that the original implementation timeline experienced delays due to database challenges and organizational issues within Blockworks
* Community members and stakeholders had been waiting for delivery of the dashboard following the approved proposal
* Core team members recently re-engaged with the Blockworks team to review project status and future execution plans
* Blockworks reportedly acknowledged the delays and apologized for the missed timeline expectations
* Revised implementation timeline now expected to run from 2026 through 2027
* Initial version of the [analytics dashboard](https://blockworks.com/analytics/akash) has now been delivered for review
* Tyler Wright encouraged all SIG Economics members to review the dashboard and provide detailed feedback
* Feedback requested around data presentation, storytelling quality, usability, and investor-focused communication
* Tyler Wright explained that the dashboard’s primary goal is to communicate the Akash growth story to investors and enterprise users
* Dashboard intended to showcase roadmap progress, ecosystem maturity, product growth, and long-term network potential
* Isaac Grooms and Tyler Wright both reviewed the initial dashboard implementation and shared early feedback with Blockworks
* Initial feedback indicated that Version 1 of the dashboard resembled the existing stats page too closely
* Recommendation provided to Blockworks focused on improving storytelling and using data more strategically to communicate ecosystem growth narratives
* Tyler Wright emphasized that dashboards should inspire confidence among investors and showcase long-term network value
* Community members were encouraged to review the dashboard during the following week and submit additional ideas, requested metrics, and usability improvements
* Tyler Wright confirmed that all feedback collected through Discord and SIG Economics channels would be shared directly with the Blockworks team
* Mentioned that future SIG Economics meetings may include direct participation from Blockworks representatives to discuss progress and implementation updates
