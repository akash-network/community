## Burn Mint Equilibrium Update

### Overview and Purpose

* Cheng Wang reiterated that BME realigns value capture directly with compute consumption.
* BME restores AKT as the canonical in-and-out asset for the network.
* Previous stable payment implementations required complex IBC routing and were suboptimal.
* Market maturity of AKT liquidity is significantly stronger than during the original stable payment rollout.

### Incentivized Testnet Phasing

* Core team has been internally testing BME for several weeks and fixing discovered bugs.
* A private group of trusted insiders has begun structured pre-testnet validation.
* Timeline for public incentivized testnet expected next week.
* Testnet duration estimated at two to three weeks.

### Testing Phases

* BME logic must be tested in isolation.
* Circuit breaker mechanism must be tested independently.
* Circuit breakers simulate extreme price volatility by manually injecting price changes.
* Smart contract component that validates oracle price feeds must also be tested separately.
* Final phase merges BME logic and oracle smart contract for full integration testing.

### Risk Mitigation

* Circuit breakers prevent runaway economic instability during sharp price drops.
* Minted compute token is non-transferable, reducing systemic risk.
* In extreme cases, settlement can default back to AKT-only mode.
