* **Console Issues and Network Upgrade Impact**

  * **Rodri R** raised concerns about recent console issues, including the inability to close deployments prior to the network upgrade.
  * Rodri noted community reports where users could not see deployments or account balances in the console.
  * Rodri also mentioned that the console continued to show **maintenance** and **read-only mode banners** after the upgrade.
  * **Scott Carruthers** explained that the recent network upgrade was intended to fix the deployment closure issue.
  * Scott clarified that lingering banners were due to **backend work still being completed** by the console team.
  * Scott stated that backend work was expected to conclude shortly and that banners should be removed once complete.

---

* **Shell Console Access Issues**

  * **Benjamin B** reported an increase in community complaints related to **shell console access**, primarily via the web console.
  * Benjamin noted uncertainty over whether the issue originated from the console interface or the underlying shell service.
  * **Scott Carruthers** shared that he had not experienced shell access issues, noting his heavy reliance on the CLI.
  * Scott confirmed that **CLI-based shell access appeared stable** and unaffected.
  * Scott committed to tracking the issue and ensuring the console team is aware of the reports.
  * **Rodri R** confirmed that CLI usage remained stable for him and reiterated that issues seemed isolated to the console.
  * Scott stated that shell access issues were unlikely to be directly related to the recent network upgrade but requested continued feedback post-upgrade.

---

* **Trials Status and Console Updates**

  * Scott provided an update on **trials being temporarily disabled** due to account conversion work by the console team.
  * He noted that trials were expected to be re-enabled shortly.
  * Scott stated that announcements would be made via Discord once trials were fully restored.
  * **Rodri R** confirmed during the meeting that deployment closure was now functioning correctly.
  * Rodri reported seeing multiple banners in the console, alternating between maintenance mode and blockchain unavailable.
  * Scott acknowledged the observation and advised waiting for the official console team announcement.

---

* **Infrastructure Roadmap: Bare Metal and VM Support**

  * Scott introduced ongoing work on **bare metal support** for the Akash network.
  * Explained that customer demand, particularly for **AI model training**, is driving the need for full host-level control.
  * Scott shared that a viable platform for delivering bare metal support has already been identified.
  * Noted that the initiative is well into the **proof-of-concept stage**.
  * Stated that bare metal support is expected to be delivered first, followed by **traditional VM support**.
  * Indicated that delivery is anticipated within the next quarter, with potential earlier availability.
  * Highlighted bare metal and VM support as significant enhancements to the Akash ecosystem.

---

* **Provider Issue: Non-Bid Transactions Impacting Bidding**

  * Scott raised awareness of a known provider-side issue affecting bid submission.
  * Explained that if a provider performed **non-bid transactions** such as withdrawing funds, bids would stop being written on-chain.
  * Clarified that provider logs would still show bidding activity even though bids were not recorded on the blockchain.
  * Noted that providers had to restart their provider pods to restore normal operation.
  * Scott confirmed that the issue has now been fixed.
  * Announced that a **new provider release** would be published shortly.
  * Advised providers to upgrade once the release is announced.

---

* **Open Discussion and Closing Remarks**

  * Scott opened the floor for additional questions or discussion topics.
  * No additional issues were raised by participants.
  * Scott noted that further provider-specific discussion would continue in the upcoming **SIG Providers call**.
  * Confirmed that the meeting transcript would be reviewed and shared.
  * Thanked all participants and formally closed the meeting.
