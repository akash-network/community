# Meeting Notes

## Opening Remarks

* Tyler opened the meeting by welcoming participants and giving context for SIG Analytics.
* SIG Analytics meets **once every four months**, due to the slower iteration cycle for analytics-related infrastructure.
* Historical background:

  * The SIG originally formed when the only Akash indexer was **closed-source**.
  * Over the past few years the indexer has become **fully open-source**, with continuous community-driven improvements.
  * Many contributors now use the indexer and Akash Analytics for research, dashboards, and network monitoring.

---

# Analytics, Indexer & Community Research Updates

### Current Role of the Indexer

* The indexer continues to serve as the backbone for:

  * Analytics dashboards
  * Research projects
  * Exploratory studies on marketplace behavior
* Multiple teams actively leverage it for data extraction and analysis.

### Research Initiatives

* Blockworks and other contributors previously explored advanced analytics:

  * Expanded indexer fields
  * Deeper on-chain economic analysis
  * Experimental research proposals

### Why Research Efforts Have Paused

* Due to the **current community pool state** and **other high-priority roadmap items**, the community does not feel this is the right time to allocate resources to expanded analytics initiatives.
* Research proposals remain paused until:

  * The community pool stabilizes
  * Governance and roadmap priorities support additional analytical initiatives

---

# Contribution Paths for the Community

### Opportunities for Contributors

Tyler highlighted several ways builders can get involved:

* Review the **Console repository** to examine the indexer codebase.
* Identify **missing data fields** or gaps in current analytics.
* Drop ideas or requests into the **SIG Analytics Discord channel**.
* Collaborate with other analytics contributors or integrators.

### Encouragement for Broader Participation

* If members know anyone interested in analytics, they are encouraged to guide them toward the SIG Analytics channel.
* This SIG acts as the coordination point for:

  * Indexer data model needs
  * Stats page enhancements
  * Research-oriented analysis discussions

---

# Meeting Cadence & Future Direction

### Quarterly Cadence Maintained

* Unless major analytics work emerges, the SIG will continue to meet **once every four months**.
* If substantial momentum builds or a new analytics initiative is needed:

  * A **dedicated working group** will be created
  * Meeting frequency would increase

### Async Collaboration Encouraged

* Members should continue posting questions, ideas, and analytics findings in Discord for ongoing asynchronous discussion.
