### General User Experience Improvements

# Home Node Product Overview

### Purpose

* Aimed at simplifying provider setup for users with consumer-grade GPUs (e.g., 4090, 5090).
* Designed as a **client-side provisioning tool** to help users become Akash providers with minimal complexity.

### Current Status

* Beta approaching soon.
* Heavy testing underway for:

  * Small and large cluster configurations
  * Hardware variance across organizations
* Will continue discussions in SIG Providers, but noted here because it is a **client-facing tool**.
